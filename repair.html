How we work

Step 1

Please make an appointment by contacting us by phone, email, or using our online booking form. Give specifics about your car and the problems you are having.

Step 2

Together with providing any required pre-appointment instructions, our staff will verify the time and date of your appointment. We'll also provide a rough estimate based on what information you've given.

Step 3

Arrive at our repair facility with your car at the appointed hour. If you would rather wait, we have a cozy waiting space available. Alternatively, you may make arrangements for a loaner car or courtesy bus.

Step 4

Your car will get a comprehensive check from our qualified specialists. After that, we will offer a thorough diagnostic report along with a final cost estimate for the necessary repairs.

Step 5

Our staff will start the repairs as soon as you give the estimate your approval. When your car is prepared for pickup, we will let you know and keep you updated along the way. Following last-minute inspections, you may pick up your car and leave with assurance.
